Rock, Paper, Scissors!

The player starts off with five of each weapon: rock, paper, and scissor. After every tool you select, the computer will select a tool at the same time. Then, the winner will be identified. If you win, you steal the computer's weapon, but if you lose, the computer steals your weapon. The first one to lose all their weapons loses! The table will update with each round. Remember, you cannot use one weapon three times in a row - it will break!. Don't try to speed click. The buttons will not work after you select a choice.

Good luck! You can click on "Directions" to see these rules again!
